# OneNotes Store Listing (English)

## Short Description

Private, secure, and fluid handwriting notes with stylus support, infinite canvas, encrypted backup, and premium tools.

## Full Description

OneNotes is a local-first Android note-taking app designed for handwriting, idea organization, and long-term project building. It is built around three principles: **smooth writing, easy finding, and data safety** — ideal for class notes, meeting records, draft reasoning, reading excerpts, daily planning, and creative work.

Write freely on an infinite canvas with zoom, pan, and multi-page support. Use different brushes, paper templates, and paper colors to match your note-taking style. The app supports stylus input with pressure sensitivity and is optimized for tablets, landscape mode, and multi-touch gestures for natural, stable writing over long sessions.

OneNotes prioritizes local privacy and data security. Note content, local accounts, premium activation data, backups, and sync-related data are stored encrypted wherever possible. You can also protect important projects with individual passwords and use encrypted backup and restore to safeguard your data.

To boost productivity, OneNotes provides project thumbnails, home screen widgets, recycle bin, search, sorting, archiving, share image generation, smart view tags, stroke playback, stroke heatmap, and writing optimization. Whether you need to capture a quick thought or organize complex ideas, you can always get back to what matters faster.

Premium unlocks more brushes, paper templates, custom paper colors, precision zoom, project passwords, WebDAV encrypted sync, creative insights, and premium share images. New users can try a 7-day Premium trial after saving a local account.

OneNotes does not pursue complex social features. Instead, it focuses on writing experience, stability, privacy protection, and long-term usability. It is for people who want to keep their handwritten content safely local while enjoying the efficiency of a modern note-taking tool.
